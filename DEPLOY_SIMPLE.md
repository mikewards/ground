# 🚀 Super Simple Deployment

## Easiest Method: Netlify Drag & Drop

**No dashboard, no tabs, just drag and drop!**

1. **Open this folder in Finder**: `/Users/ed/Desktop/P1`
2. **Go to**: https://app.netlify.com/drop
3. **Drag the entire `P1` folder** onto that page
4. **Wait 30 seconds**
5. **Done!** Your site is live

That's it! No configuration, no tabs, no complexity.

---

## Alternative: GitHub Pages (Also Super Simple)

1. Go to: https://github.com/wardmic4/flow-platform/settings/pages
2. Under "Source", select: **Deploy from a branch**
3. Branch: **main**
4. Folder: **/** (root)
5. Click **Save**
6. Wait 2 minutes
7. Your site will be at: `https://wardmic4.github.io/flow-platform/`

**Note:** You'll need to update all your links to use `/flow-platform/` as the base path, or we can set up a custom domain.
